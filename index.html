Tech Jobs Academy

An intensive, 16-week technical training program preparing underemployed and unemployed New Yorkers for in-demand tech jobs in cloud and server administration.

OVERVIEW

Tech Jobs Academy is a collaboration between Microsoft Corporation, the NYC Tech Talent Pipeline and the City University of New York (CUNY). In its pilot year, these partners have joined together to launch Tech Jobs Academy within CUNYâ€™s New York City College of Technology, a technical training program that targets underemployed and unemployed New Yorkers who are passionate about technology and ready to launch a new career in the field.

Tech Jobs Academy students will spend 16 weeks developing highly sought-after skills in cloud and server administration, as well as building professional skills to help them land a job upon completion. This pilot program will serve twenty-five motivated New Yorkers.

APPLICATION PROCESS

  1. Check your eligibility

    Do you meet our eligibility criteria? You'll be asked to present proof of eligibility (including tax returns to verify income), so make sure you qualify.

  2. Fill out application form

    The application helps us get to know you better. Answer questions about your interest in technology and what gets you excited about the program.

  3. Pass tech assessment

    Learn network fundamentals and take an online entrance exam to prove your knowledge.

  4. Take math and reading test

    Take an assessment to show your math skills (at least 11th grade level on TABE test) and reading proficiency (at least 10th grade level on TABE test)

  5. Interview

    If selected, you will be invited to interview for a spot in the cohort.

Eligibility Requirements

  • High school diploma or GED
  • At least 18 years old
  • Less than a 4-year degree
  • Unemployed or underemployed *
  • NYC resident
  • Legally allowed to work in the U.S.
  • Able to commit to full time training (minimum 40 hours per week)
  • Available for employment immediately upon graduation
  • Meet all federal selective service requirements, if applicable

    • * less than $50K annual salary (individual)
